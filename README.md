# GDP-Analysis-Using-ML-Models
This project focuses on analyzing and forecasting Gross Domestic Product (GDP) using Machine Learning (ML) models. GDP is a key economic indicator that reflects the economic health of a country. By applying ML techniques, this project aims to identify patterns, relationships, and trends in economic data to improve prediction accuracy 
To analyze India’s Gross Domestic Product (GDP) and assess the overall pattern of economic growth over the past decades.
To evaluate the contribution of major sectors such as agriculture, industry, and services to India’s GDP..
To identify key growth drivers that have influenced India’s economic expansion..
To examine major challenges like unemployment, income inequality, inflation, and dependence on external trade.
To highlight factors that may gives long-term economic stability and balanced development.
